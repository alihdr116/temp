Our Logistics Services

Professional Dispatch Services

At Route Rise Logistics, dispatching is more than just booking loads. We act as your back-office partner, allowing you to focus on driving while we handle the rest:

Load Searching & Booking

We find the best freight opportunities that fit your equipment type, preferred routes, and rate expectations. Our dispatchers negotiate on your behalf to secure profitable loads every time.

Rate Negotiation

We communicate directly with brokers and shippers to negotiate the best possible rates, ensuring you maximize your earnings on every trip.

Paperwork & Compliance

We handle rate confirmations, bills of lading, and ensure compliance with FMCSA regulations so you can stay stress-free on the road.

24/7 Driver Support

Our dispatch team is available around the clock to assist with route planning, updates, and any issues that may arise during transit.

Factoring & Invoicing Assistance

We work with factoring companies and assist in preparing invoices so that you get paid on time without financial stress.

Dedicated Carrier Relations

We build long-term partnerships with our carriers, learning your preferences to provide consistent freight and trusted support.

Equipment We Dispatch

We specialize in dispatching across multiple equipment types to keep your trucks loaded and profitable.

Box Truck

Perfect for regional and last-mile freight. We connect box truck operators with steady loads such as retail, furniture, and parcel deliveries.

Reefer

Expert dispatch for refrigerated trailers, keeping temperature-sensitive freight like food, beverages, and pharmaceuticals moving safely and on time.

Car Hauler

Specialized dispatch services for single and multi-car haulers, ensuring secure transportation and optimized scheduling.

Power Only

We connect power units with trailers from brokers and shippers nationwide, giving you flexibility and constant work opportunities.

Dry Van

The most versatile freight option. We help dry van operators with general cargo, retail goods, and nationwide routes at strong rates.

Flatbed

Specialized dispatch for oversized and heavy loads, including construction materials, machinery, and industrial freight.
